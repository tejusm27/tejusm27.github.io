Meet Element.

Element I, our prototype, is currently being developed and is meeting our benchmarks. 

Water Production

Our current device makes 1 gallon / hr. With scaling, we predict to make 100 gal/day.

Energy Efficiency

Element is 4.5 times more energy efficient than traditional condensation. Element works on a 12 V or 110 V system.

Who needs one?

Although everyone needs clean drinking water, we have identified certain customers that can benefit from an AtmoSpark unit.

  • Boats
  • RVs
  • Homeowners
  • NGOs

Reliable

Unlike traditional water makers the relatively simple design means that Element will break down less and need less maintenance, meaning you won’t be stranded at sea with no water.

Portable

Unlike traditional water makers, Element is portable, meaning you can transport it on and off your boat and you don’t have to pay installation fees or worry about pickling.

Fuel Efficient

Carrying an Element device is more fuel efficient than filling a water tank because it's lighter, helping you go longer without refueling.

Space Efficient

An Element unit can be mounted on the roof of your RV, meaning that space inside your RV is conserved.

Perfect for Disaster Weather

When weather conditions make it difficult to access clean drinking water, you don't have to worry about storing bottled water. An Element unit can be powered by a generator or solar panels if traditional power isn't available.

Bottled Water Quality

Bottled water quality without the plastic waste associated with plastic bottles. Also, AtmoSpark generated water can be used to cook and make a great cup of coffee.

Global

Most countries with water shortages have the humidity required to generate water efficiently from the atmosphere.

Affordable

We plan to partner with NGOs to help provide an affordable water solution to everyone. If you're interested, please contact us.

FAQ

 Does water quality depend on air quality?

No, our technology targets only the water molecules in the air, unlike condensation, so we get 100% pure water regardless of conditions.

If it's so pure, why do I need a filter?

Unlike traditional carbon filters, our filters are additive mineral cartridges-- the water that comes out of the unit is so pure it is distilled; therefore not drinkable unless more minerals are added in.

 What if I want distilled water?

Although our mission is to provide drinking water for people who don’t have access to drinking water, we have considered providing distilled water, but 1) there is not as much demand for distilled water as pure drinking water and 2) we decided to stay true to our mission.

However, if you need a distilled water generator, feel free to contact us. 

 Can it only be used in really humid climates?

Our technology works anywhere that is above 40% relative humidity. This means that the only place Element wont work is the desert and the mountains.

If you're concerned the unit won't work in your area, ask us.
